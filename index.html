Co-authored multiple additional publications as part of the broader collaboration.

Firecrown

Multi-probe cosmological likelihood framework for the LSST DESC. Led development and maintenance of this core analysis tool.

TJPCov

Covariance matrix computation pipeline for two-point correlation functions. Essential infrastructure for survey cosmology.
